Welcome to Catchphrases!

Catchphrases is an exciting word-guessing game that tests your quick thinking and communication skills. The objective of the game is to guess the word or phrase based on the clues given to you. Gather your friends, select a category, and take turns describing the phrases without using the actual words! Race against the clock to see how many phrases your team can guess before time runs out. Get ready for a fun-filled and fast-paced game that will keep you entertained for hours!
